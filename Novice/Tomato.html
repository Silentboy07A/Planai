Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum)

Tomatoes are one of the most popular garden vegetables, known for their juicy, flavorful fruit. They require full sun and regular watering for best results.

  • Water: Deep watering 1-2 times per week, allowing soil to dry between watering.
  • Light: Full sun, at least 6-8 hours daily.
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil rich in organic matter.
  • Temperature: Warm conditions, with ideal temperatures between 70-85°F (21-29°C).
  • Growth: Encourage staking or caging for support as plant grows.

Tips: Mulch around plants to retain moisture and prevent weeds. Avoid overhead watering to reduce disease risk.

Common Problems: Be cautious of pests like aphids, tomato hornworms, and fungal diseases.
