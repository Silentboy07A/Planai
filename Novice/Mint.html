Mint (Mentha)

Mint is a fragrant herb known for its refreshing aroma and culinary uses. It thrives in sunny locations with moist soil and is easy to grow in gardens or pots.

Water: Keep soil consistently moist; water regularly but avoid waterlogging.
Light: Full sun to partial shade.
Soil: Well-draining, rich organic soil.
Temperature: Prefers moderate temperatures, sensitive to frost.

Mint can spread aggressively; consider planting in pots or controlled areas to manage growth.
