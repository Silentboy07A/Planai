Marigold (Tagetes)

Marigolds are bright, cheerful flowers that are easy to grow and maintain. They thrive in sunlight and are known for their pest-repellent properties.

  • Water: Moderate watering; keep soil moist but well-drained.
  • Light: Full sun is ideal for lush blooms.
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil preferred.
  • Temperature: Warm weather loving; tolerate mild drought.
  • Growth: Fast-growing with vibrant orange, yellow, or red flowers.

Tips: Deadhead spent blooms to encourage continuous flowering.

Common Problems: Watch for fungal diseases in overly wet soil and pest infestations like spider mites.
