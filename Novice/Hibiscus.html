Hibiscus (Hibiscus rosa-sinensis)

Hibiscus is a beautiful flowering plant known for its large, colorful blooms that thrive in warm climates and bright light.

  • Water: Water regularly to keep soil moist but not waterlogged.
  • Light: Full sun to partial shade for best flowering.
  • Soil: Well-draining, fertile soil rich in organic matter.
  • Temperature: Prefers warm climates; protect from frost.
  • Growth: Produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, orange, and yellow.

Tips: Prune after flowering to encourage bushier growth and more blooms.

Common Problems: Watch for aphids, spider mites, and fungal diseases. Proper watering and air circulation help prevent problems.
