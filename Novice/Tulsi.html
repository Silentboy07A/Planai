Tulsi (Ocimum sanctum)

Tulsi, also known as holy basil, is an aromatic, sacred herb revered in many cultures. It thrives in tropical to subtropical climates and is used both for medicinal purposes and culinary flavoring.

  • Water: Moderate watering; allow the top soil to dry before watering again.
  • Light: Full sun to partial shade.
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil.
  • Temperature: Warm, frost-free conditions preferred.
  • Growth: Medium growth rate with lush green leaves and small purple flowers.

Tips: Prune regularly to encourage bushy growth and higher yield of leaves.

Common Problems: Watch for pests like aphids and fungal diseases especially in humid environments.
