Spinach (Spinacia oleracea)

Spinach is a nutrient-rich leafy green vegetable grown in temperate climates. It grows quickly and thrives in cool weather.

  • Water: Keep soil moist but not waterlogged; regular watering is important.
  • Light: Partial shade to full sun.
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil rich in organic matter.
  • Temperature: Prefers cooler temperatures between 50-70°F (10-21°C).
  • Growth: Fast-growing leaves, harvested regularly for best quality.

Tips: Sow seeds directly into soil and thin seedlings to avoid crowding.

Common Problems: Leaf miner insects and fungal diseases; ensure good airflow and crop rotation.
