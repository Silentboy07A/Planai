Guava (Psidium guajava)

Guava is a tropical fruit tree known for its nutritious and delicious fruit. It prefers well-drained soil and warm climates.

  • Water: Water regularly, especially during dry periods.
  • Light: Full sun for best fruit production.
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil with good organic content.
  • Temperature: Thrives in tropical to subtropical climates.
  • Growth: Moderate growth; prune to maintain shape.

Tips: Fertilize twice a year and mulch to retain moisture.

Common Problems: Watch for fruit flies, aphids, and root rot.
