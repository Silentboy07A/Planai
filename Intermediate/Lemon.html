Lemon (Citrus limon)

Lemon is a popular citrus fruit tree valued for its fragrant blossoms and sour fruit. It requires warm climates and regular watering.

  • Water: Water deeply and regularly, especially during fruit development.
  • Light: Full sun for healthy growth and fruiting.
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil with organic matter.
  • Temperature: Prefers warm and frost-free conditions.
  • Growth: Moderate growth rate with fragrant flowers and bright yellow fruit.

Tips: Use mulch and balanced fertilizer to enhance health and fruit production.

Common Problems: Watch out for aphids, citrus leaf miners, and root rot.
