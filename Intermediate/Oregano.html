Oregano (Origanum vulgare)

Oregano is a fragrant herb used widely in cooking and known for its hardy growth. It thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-drained soil.

  • Water: Water moderately; let soil dry out between watering.
  • Light: Full sun.
  • Soil: Well-drained, sandy or loamy soil.
  • Temperature: Prefers warm temperatures; avoid frost.
  • Growth: Compact, bushy growth with aromatic leaves.

Tips: Prune regularly to prevent legginess and encourage dense growth.

Common Problems: Watch for root rot caused by overwatering.
