Rosemary (Rosmarinus officinalis)

Rosemary is a fragrant evergreen herb with needle-like leaves, used for cooking and ornamental purposes. It prefers warm climates and well-drained soil.

  • Water: Water moderately, allowing soil to dry between watering.
  • Light: Full sun for best growth.
  • Soil: Well-drained sandy or loamy soil.
  • Temperature: Prefers temperate to warm climates; sensitive to frost.
  • Growth: Slow to moderate growth; prune regularly to maintain shape.

Tips: Avoid overwatering to prevent root rot, and harvest leaves regularly to encourage new growth.

Common Problems: Watch for spider mites and powdery mildew.
