Jasmine (Jasminum spp.)

Jasmine is a fragrant flowering plant known for its delicate white or yellow blossoms. It thrives in warm climates and prefers well-drained soil.

  • Water: Water regularly, keeping soil moist but not waterlogged.
  • Light: Full sun to partial shade.
  • Soil: Well-draining, fertile soil rich in organic matter.
  • Temperature: Prefers warm and frost-free environments.
  • Growth: Can be trained as a shrub or vine with regular pruning.

Tips: Prune after flowering to promote healthy growth and bloom cycles.

Common Problems: Watch for aphids, spider mites, and fungal diseases.
