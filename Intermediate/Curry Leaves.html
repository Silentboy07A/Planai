Curry Leaves (Murraya koenigii)

Curry leaves plant is valued for its aromatic leaves used widely in cooking. It prefers warm climates and regular watering.

  • Water: Keep soil consistently moist, but do not overwater.
  • Light: Best in partial sun to filtered light.
  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil enriched with organic matter.
  • Temperature: Thrives in warm, frost-free conditions.
  • Growth: Moderate growth rate, prune regularly to encourage bushiness.

Tips: Use well-drained pots and fertilize during growing months.

Common Problems: Guard against aphids, scale insects, and root rot from excess moisture.
