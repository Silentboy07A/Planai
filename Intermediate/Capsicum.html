Capsicum (Capsicum annuum)

Capsicum, commonly known as bell pepper or chili pepper, is a versatile vegetable grown worldwide. It prefers warm weather, ample sunlight, and regular watering.

  • Water: Water regularly, keeping soil consistently moist but not soggy.
  • Light: Full sun, minimum 6 hours daily.
  • Soil: Well-drained fertile soil rich in organic matter.
  • Temperature: Warm temperature between 70-85°F (21-29°C).
  • Growth: Plants may require staking or support as they mature.

Tips: Mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds, and harvest fruits when fully colored.

Common Problems: Monitor for aphids, whiteflies, and fungal diseases.
