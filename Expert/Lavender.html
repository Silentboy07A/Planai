Lavender (Lavandula)

Lavender is a fragrant and hardy flowering plant prized for its purple blooms and aromatic foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils.

  • Water: Water moderately, allowing soil to dry out between watering.
  • Light: Full sun preferred for best bloom production.
  • Soil: Sandy or loamy, well-drained soil with neutral to slightly alkaline pH.
  • Temperature: Thrives in temperate climates; drought tolerant once established.
  • Growth: Slow to moderate growth; prune after flowering to maintain shape.

Tips: Regular pruning improves bushiness and prolongs flowering season.

Common Problems: Root rot if overwatered; watch for aphids and fungal diseases.
