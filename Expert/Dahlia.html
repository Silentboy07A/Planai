Dahlia

Dahlia is a tuberous perennial known for its showy flowers that come in various colors and shapes. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

  • Water: Water deeply but allow soil to dry between watering.
  • Light: Full sun required for abundant blooms.
  • Soil: Rich, well-draining soil amended with organic matter.
  • Temperature: Prefers mild to warm climates; protect from frost.
  • Growth: Grown from tubers; stake tall varieties for support.

Tips: Deadhead spent flowers to encourage continual blooming.

Common Problems: Watch for powdery mildew, slugs, and aphids.
