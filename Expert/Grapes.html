Grapes

Grapes are deciduous woody vines prized for their sweet fruit and wine production. They prefer well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

  • Water: Water regularly, reducing frequency during dormancy.
  • Light: Require full sun for quality fruit.
  • Soil: Sandy-loam, well-drained soil with pH 5.5-6.5.
  • Temperature: Thrive in temperate climates with warm summers.
  • Growth: Require training and pruning for optimal yield.

Tips: Use trellises and regular pruning to keep vines healthy and productive.

Common Problems: Monitor for powdery mildew, downy mildew, and grape berry moth.
