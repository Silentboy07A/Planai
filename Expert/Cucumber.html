Cucumber

Cucumber is a fast-growing vine known for its elongated, edible fruits. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

  • Water: Maintain consistent moisture but avoid waterlogging.
  • Light: Full sun for vigorous growth.
  • Soil: Fertile, well-drained soil with good organic content.
  • Temperature: Prefers warm temperatures between 70-95°F (21-35°C).
  • Growth: Needs support like trellises; prune runners to focus energy on fruit.

Tips: Mulch to retain soil moisture and reduce weeds, and harvest fruits young for best taste.

Common Problems: Watch for powdery mildew, aphids, and cucumber beetles.
