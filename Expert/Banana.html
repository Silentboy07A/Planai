Banana

Banana is a fast-growing tropical plant known for its large edible fruit. It requires warm temperatures, high humidity, and rich, well-drained soil.

  • Water: Requires regular and plentiful watering to maintain consistent moisture.
  • Light: Prefers full sun but can tolerate partial shade.
  • Soil: Deep, fertile, well-drained soil with good organic content.
  • Temperature: Thrives in temperatures between 78-86°F (26-30°C).
  • Growth: Large herbaceous plant; protect from strong winds and frost.

Tips: Mulch heavily to retain moisture, and fertilize regularly for best growth and fruiting.

Common Problems: Watch for nematodes, banana weevils, and fungal infections.
