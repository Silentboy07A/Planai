Onion (Allium cepa)

Onion (Allium cepa) is a widely cultivated bulb vegetable known for its pungent flavor and medicinal properties. It prefers cool to moderate climates and well-drained soil.

  • Water: Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.
  • Light: Requires full sun for optimal growth.
  • Soil: Well-drained loamy soil rich in organic matter.
  • Temperature: Optimal growth at 55-75°F (13-24°C).
  • Growth: Bulb forms underground, needs regular care and fertilization.

Tips: Mulching helps retain moisture and control weeds. Use balanced fertilizer during growing season.

Common Problems: Watch for onion maggots, downy mildew, and neck rot.
