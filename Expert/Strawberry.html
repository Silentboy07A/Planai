Strawberry (Fragaria × ananassa)

Strawberry is a popular fruiting plant known for its sweet, juicy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers cool to mild climates.

  • Water: Keep soil evenly moist but not waterlogged.
  • Light: Full sun preferred for best fruit production.
  • Soil: Well-draining, fertile soil rich in organic matter.
  • Temperature: Prefers cool to mild temperatures between 60-80°F (15-27°C).
  • Growth: Spreading runners; requires space for healthy development.

Tips: Mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds, and remove old leaves to prevent disease.

Common Problems: Check for pests like slugs and diseases like powdery mildew.
